We offer a wide variety of catering options, including buffet-style, plated, and family-style meals. Our expert chefs can customize menus to meet your specific needs and dietary restrictions.

Food Options

We offer a variety of delicious food options.

Buffet

Our buffet-style meals offer a wide variety of options, allowing your guests to customize their plates and try a little bit of everything. Perfect for larger events and weddings.

Plated

Our plated meals are perfect for a more formal event, with each guest receiving their own individually plated meal. Choose from our selection of entrees and sides.

Family-style

Our family-style meals are perfect for a more casual event, with large platters of food shared amongst guests. Choose from our selection of entrees and sides.
